Feb 3 – Mar 3, 2026

This report covers numoya's Google Ads performance from Feb 3 – Mar 3, 2026. The account generated $420,284 in revenue from $116,126 in spend, delivering a 3.62x ROAS across 8,505 conversions. In the concluded week (Feb 25 – Mar 4), spend fell 14% week-over-week to $29,476 — but efficiency improved: ROAS climbed to 2.94x and CPA dropped to $16.48. The volume pullback appears algorithm-driven rather than demand-led, as CTR jumped 62% and clicks grew 31%. Key priorities are fixing 9 POOR-rated ads in Brand Search US, scaling the high-performing "numoya go brush pro" keyword ($11.74 CPA over the period), and correcting a brand misspelling in the product feed. Two PMAX campaigns (UK/NZ, score 26.8, and Dryer, score 34.0) require urgent review due to collapsed concluded-week performance.
